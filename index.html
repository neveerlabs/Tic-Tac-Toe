Privacy Policy for Tic-Tac-Toe AI

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Neverlabs ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our Tic-Tac-Toe AI application.

2. Information We Collect

Our application operates entirely in your browser and does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

3. Local Storage (IndexedDB)

The application uses browser IndexedDB to save:

  • Game Scores: Your win/loss statistics
  • Q-Learning Table: AI training data (all past game experiences)
  • Game Parameters: Your epsilon, learning rate, and discount factor settings

This data remains on YOUR device only and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. Unlike localStorage, IndexedDB can handle large datasets efficiently, allowing the AI to store extensive game history.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Icons8: For UI icons (loaded from their CDN)
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting (if applicable)

These services may collect standard web analytics data (such as IP address and browser type). Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

5. Cookies

We do NOT use cookies. All data is stored in browser IndexedDB.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it. You can clear your data at any time by:

  • Clicking the "RESET MEMORY" button in the app (clears Q‑table)
  • Using browser developer tools to clear IndexedDB
  • Clearing your browser cache/storage

7. Children's Privacy

Our application does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (via browser developer tools → IndexedDB)
  • Delete your data (via "RESET MEMORY" button or browser settings)
  • Export your data (via "EXPORT Q" button, which downloads the Q‑table as JSON)

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of any changes.
